Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 Apr 2026

INRpal is not a medical device.
This app is a personal tracking tool only. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition. Nothing in this app is medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider before making any changes to your medication or treatment.

This Privacy Policy explains how INRpal ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. What we collect

Data you enter directly:

  • INR readings, warfarin doses, and weekly dose schedules.
  • Food and vitamin K diary entries.
  • Medication names, dosing schedules, and adherence logs.
  • Sleep logs and daily symptom check-ins.
  • Health settings such as your INR target range and medical indication.
  • Name or nickname you choose to enter (optional).

Data read from Apple Health (optional, iOS only):

  • If you connect Apple Health, we read steps, resting heart rate, body weight, and sleep analysis. This data is displayed within the App only — it is never transmitted to our servers.
  • If you connect Apple Health, we write your INR readings back to Apple Health so they appear alongside your other health records.

Data sent to our servers (optional features):

  • Cloud backup (opt-in): If you sign in with your email, an encrypted copy of your logged data is sent to Supabase (our cloud provider) and associated with your account. This lets you restore your data on a new device.
  • Anonymous peer benchmarking (opt-in, signed-in users only): When cloud backup is active, we push anonymous performance metrics — your 30-day time-in-therapeutic-range (TTR) percentage, medical indication, and medication adherence streak — to enable the peer comparison feature. These metrics contain no name, email, or directly identifying information.
  • Caregiver sharing (opt-in): If you generate a share code, a limited clinical summary (latest INR value, TTR, next test date, target range) is stored on our servers and shared with whoever you give the code to.
  • AI Coach (opt-in): If you use the AI coaching feature, your question and minimal context (e.g., your latest INR value and trend summary) are sent to our AI gateway to generate a response.

2. How we use your information

  • Provide core features: logging, charts, trend analysis, and exports.
  • Display and restore your data across devices (cloud backup).
  • Power anonymous peer benchmarking so you can see how your TTR compares.
  • Share a clinical summary with a caregiver or family member you choose.
  • Generate AI coaching responses to your questions.
  • Respond to support requests you initiate.

We do not use your health data for marketing, advertising, or any purpose unrelated to your personal health management.

3. Where your data is stored

By default, all data is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

If you choose to enable cloud backup, your data is encrypted in transit and stored securely by Supabase (supabase.com), a third-party cloud infrastructure provider. Supabase processes data solely to store and return it to you and is bound by contract to handle it securely. Your cloud data is protected by row-level security and is only accessible with your email account.

Apple Health data (steps, heart rate, sleep, weight) is read directly from your device and is never sent to our servers.

4. Third-party services

  • Supabase (supabase.com): Cloud backup, authentication, anonymous peer metrics, and caregiver data storage.
  • AI gateway (ww-ai-proxy.vercel.app): Processes AI coach prompts. Your prompts are not used to train AI models. The gateway is contractually restricted from using your content for advertising or unrelated purposes.

We do not sell your data. We do not use advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or data brokers.

5. Apple Health and HealthKit

INRpal integrates with Apple Health under Apple's HealthKit guidelines. Specifically:

  • Health data read from HealthKit is used only to display context within the App and is never shared with third parties or used for advertising.
  • INR readings you log are written to Apple Health for your personal records.
  • HealthKit data is not stored in iCloud by INRpal.

6. Legal bases (EEA / UK)

  • Performance of a contract: to provide the App features you use.
  • Consent: cloud backup, peer benchmarking, caregiver sharing, and AI coach are all opt-in.
  • Legitimate interests: maintaining the security and stability of the App.

7. Your rights and how to delete your data

  • Delete individual entries: Tap and hold any INR reading, meal, or log entry within the App to delete it.
  • Delete your cloud account: Go to More → Backup & Sync → "Delete account". This permanently deletes your cloud backup and Supabase account. Your local data on this device is not affected.
  • Revoke caregiver access: Go to More → Caregiver Access and revoke your share code at any time.
  • Remove all local data: Uninstalling the App from your device removes all locally stored data.
  • Depending on your location, you may also have rights to access, correct, or object to processing of your data. Contact us at the address below to exercise these rights.

8. Data retention

Local data is retained until you delete it or uninstall the App. Cloud backup data is retained until you delete your account. Anonymous peer metrics are retained while your account exists and removed when your account is deleted.

9. Security

We use encryption in transit (HTTPS), row-level security on our database, and device-level secure storage for your authentication session. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children's privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. International transfers

Supabase may process data outside your country. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses) to protect your information.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in the App or legal requirements. We'll update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, provide notice within the App.
